CV

Short bio

I am a registred statistician at the CONRE2, but I would rather consider myself an applied Bayesian statistician working on public health problems. I am currently based in London working as a research fellow at the LSHTM, UK, and I also hold permanent position at Fiocruz, Brazil. My main research interests are on developing Bayesian statistical solutions/models to applied real problems, usually research questions for infectious diseases.
