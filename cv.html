Short bio

I hold permanent position at Scientific Computing Program PROCC, Fiocruz, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I consider myself an applied Bayesian biostatistician devoting my work to help solving problems on infectious diseases surveillance. I am a registred statistician at the CONRE2. And I spent some time in London working infectious disease modelling at the CMMID/LSHTM, UK. My main research interests are on developing Bayesian statistical solutions/models to applied real problems, usually research questions related to infectious diseases epidemics.
